About Saad




A Journey of Impactful Mission


Saad Siddiqui, a 19 years old ambitious Social Entrepreneur. He has been awarded the youngest Pakistani Entrepreneur award 2020. He has also been the recipient of numerous other such accolades, including IBA Startups Competition 2021 winner award. He has also served as a Brand Ambassador for IBA Aman Centre for Entrepreneurial Development, Rizq, and LUMS Entrepreneurial society; as well as the current Country Head Pakistan for World Literacy Foundation. His startup project, ‘Then Foundation’ is a revolutionary plan to provide accessible education through technology, and he is also the Co-founder of The Marketects Co, a Digital Marketing Agency to help startups in building their businesses.
